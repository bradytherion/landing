Peruvian Reel

Description

"Peruvian Reel" is a motion graphics project created in After Effects, showcasing the talent and energy of the 11 players of the Peruvian national football team.

In this project, I focused on blending motion design with the powerful narratives of each player, emphasizing their unique qualities while celebrating the unity and strength of the Peruvian squad. The result is a visually engaging piece that honors the players and their contribution to the nation's football legacy.
